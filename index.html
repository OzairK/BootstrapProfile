About

Ozair Khan is an engineer with experience in the Oil and Gas and Power Utility industry. Ozair takes pride in the work he does, and believes in empowering his fellow team members to help create a strong collaborative environment. He has worked with Halliburton Energy Services as a Field Engineer for 3.5 years, where he lead his team to an excellent customer service record and minimal NPT during a demanding time in the industry. Ozair has taken his FE exam, and has his EIT license. He holds a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin.He has recently completed a Web Development Bootcamp offered through UT Austin and acquired mastery over the following skills: JavaScript, jQuery, React.js, GIT, Github, Firebase, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Media Queries, APIs, JSON, AJAX, MySQL, Sequelize, MongoDB, Node, Express, Passport.js, the command line, C, C++, Java, computer science fundamentals, writing tests, and Chrome Dev tools.
